Welcome to Technical Committee on Robot Control

Scope

Our scope is to facilitate a vibrant, inclusive discourse that revitalizes the field of modeling and control, empowering emerging researchers to embrace and expand upon these disciplines as they shape the future of robotics research.

Furthermore, we believe that incentivizing Modeling and Control research will enhance the inclusivity of the RAS community. Compared to other branches in robotics, M&C research demands fewer investments, enabling broader participation from groups with limited access to funding for costly equipment and computational resources.
